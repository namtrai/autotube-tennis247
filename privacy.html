Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-16

Overview

AutoTube Tennis247 is a private creator tool used by the owner to prepare, review, and upload short-form tennis news videos to the owner's own social channels.

Information We Access

When authorized through a platform such as TikTok, the app may access only the permissions granted by the account owner, such as basic account information and video upload permissions. The app is not intended for use by the general public.

How Information Is Used

Authorized access is used only to verify the account and upload videos selected or approved by the account owner. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

Access tokens and local configuration, when used, are stored privately by the account owner in local project files that are not published in this website or public repositories.

Data Deletion

The account owner can revoke platform access at any time from the relevant platform account settings or developer app settings. Local token files can also be deleted by the owner to remove stored authorization.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the owner of the associated developer account.
